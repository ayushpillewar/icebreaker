IceBreaker Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 18, 2026

IceBreaker ("we", "our", or "the app") is a Bluetooth-based local discovery and chat application. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and your rights regarding your information.

1. Information We Collect

IceBreaker collects only the minimum information needed to operate the app:

  • Display Name — A name you voluntarily provide. This name is broadcast via Bluetooth to other nearby IceBreaker users when you enable the "Visible to others" toggle. It is stored locally on your device using the device's built-in secure storage (AsyncStorage).
  • Chat Messages — Messages exchanged with nearby users are transmitted directly device-to-device over Bluetooth (peer-to-peer). They are stored only in your device's memory for the duration of the session. Messages are not sent to, or stored on, any external server.
  • Bluetooth Scan Data — The app temporarily reads Bluetooth signal data (device names, signal strength) of nearby devices to populate the "Nearby" list. This data is held only in memory and is never transmitted off your device.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your real name, email address, phone number, or any other contact information.
  • We do not collect location data (GPS coordinates).
  • We do not have user accounts or cloud synchronisation.
  • We do not use third-party analytics SDKs, advertising networks, or crash-reporting services.
  • We do not collect or share device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV) with any third party.
  • We do not track your activity across other apps or websites.

3. How Information Is Used

  • Your display name is used solely to identify you to other nearby users who have also enabled visibility.
  • Messages are used solely to facilitate real-time peer-to-peer communication between two consenting users who are physically near each other.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with any third party. Because all data stays on-device, there is no server-side processing of your data by us.

When you enable visibility, your display name becomes visible to any nearby device running IceBreaker. You can stop this at any time by toggling off "Visible to others" on the Home screen.

5. Data Retention

  • Your display name is retained on your device until you explicitly clear it within the app or uninstall the app.
  • Chat messages exist only in memory during an active session. They are not persisted to disk and are lost when the app is closed.

6. Children's Privacy

IceBreaker is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information through the app, please contact us and we will take steps to remove it.

7. Bluetooth Permission

The app requests Bluetooth permission in order to scan for and advertise to nearby devices. This permission is used exclusively for the in-app discovery and chat features. You may deny or revoke Bluetooth permission at any time in iOS Settings; doing so will prevent the discovery features from functioning.

8. Security

All communication occurs over Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE), which is a short-range (≈10 m) local wireless protocol. Communication is therefore inherently limited to people physically near you. No internet connection is required for core functionality.

9. Your Rights

Because IceBreaker stores no data on any server, there is no account to delete and no server-side data to retrieve. To remove your display name from your device, use the in-app option on the Home screen or uninstall the application. This satisfies your right to erasure under applicable privacy regulations (including GDPR and CCPA) to the extent they apply.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by updating the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, by displaying an in-app notice. Continued use of the app after such changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
